About Us

We are a non-profit organization focused on providing aid to those in need. At Up Skill, we are dedicated to empowering individuals by equipping them with practical skills that foster self-reliance and economic growth. As a non-profit organization, our mission is to provide hands-on training in various vocational fields, enabling citizens to develop expertise that enhances their employability and entrepreneurial potential. Through our structured programs in sewing, cooking, hairdressing, and other essential skills, we create opportunities for personal and professional advancement. We believe that by upskilling individuals, we contribute to stronger communities and a brighter future, where everyone has the tools to build a sustainable livelihood. Join us as we transform lives through skills and education! In today’s fast-changing world, having practical skills can open doors to new opportunities and financial independence. Our Sewing, Cooking, and Hairdressing Classes are designed to equip individuals with valuable hands-on expertise, empowering them to build careers, start businesses, or simply enhance their daily lives. Whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your abilities, our structured training sessions provide a supportive and engaging learning environment. Our upskilling program is more than just learning techniques—it’s about building confidence, self-reliance, and economic empowerment. By enrolling in these classes, individuals will not only gain practical skills but also open doors to new income-generating opportunities. Whether you aim to secure employment, start your own business, or simply improve your expertise, our training provides a solid foundation for success. Join us today and take the first step toward a brighter, more skilled future!

Our Programmes

Sewing

Our sewing classes cater to both beginners and those with some experience in tailoring. Participants will learn the basics of fabric selection, pattern cutting, stitching, and garment construction. Advanced sessions will cover professional tailoring techniques, alterations, and design customization. By the end of the course, students will have the skills to create stylish outfits, household items, or even launch their own fashion brand.

Hair dressing

The beauty industry continues to thrive, offering numerous career paths for skilled hairdressers. Our hairdressing classes cover hair care, cutting, styling, braiding, and professional salon techniques. Students will practice hands-on styling methods while learning about different hair types, product usage, and customer service. This course is ideal for anyone looking to start a salon business or enhance their hairstyling skills.

Cooking

Cooking is not just a necessity; it’s a skill that brings people together and can also be turned into a rewarding profession. Our cooking classes teach essential kitchen skills, meal planning, and diverse cooking techniques, from traditional recipes to modern cuisine. Whether aspiring to become a professional chef or simply wanting to prepare healthier, delicious meals at home, participants will gain confidence and creativity in the kitchen.
